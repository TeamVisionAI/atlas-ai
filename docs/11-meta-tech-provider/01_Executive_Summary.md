## Introduction

Atlas Platform is a cloud-based Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) platform designed to help organizations manage customer communications, operational workflows, appointment scheduling, and business activities through a secure and centralized workspace.

The platform combines communication tools, scheduling, customer management, and intelligent operational assistance into a single integrated experience.

Atlas is built to reduce administrative work, improve customer response times, and simplify daily operations while allowing organizations to maintain full ownership of their business assets and customer relationships.

---

## Purpose

The purpose of Atlas is to provide organizations with an operational platform that connects business workflows with customer communications in a secure, scalable, and policy-compliant manner.

Atlas is not a messaging service.

Atlas is an operational business platform that incorporates communication capabilities as one component of a broader workflow.

---

## Meta Platform Integration

Atlas integrates with Meta's official WhatsApp Business Platform using the Embedded Signup onboarding experience.

Each organization authorizes Atlas to access only its own Meta Business assets.

Atlas never creates or claims ownership of customer WhatsApp Business Accounts.

Organizations remain the owners of:

- Meta Business Accounts
- WhatsApp Business Accounts
- Phone Numbers
- Customer Conversations
- Business Assets

Atlas acts only as an authorized software platform after customer approval.

## Customer Value

Atlas helps organizations:

- Improve customer response times
- Organize customer communications
- Manage appointments
- Coordinate operational activities
- Reduce repetitive administrative work
- Improve visibility into daily operations

---

## Security and Privacy

Security and customer privacy are fundamental platform principles.

Atlas follows modern security practices including:

- HTTPS encryption
- Secure authentication
- Role-based authorization
- Secure webhook validation
- Audit logging
- Least-privilege access

Atlas only requests Meta permissions that are required to provide customer-requested functionality.

---

## Platform Philosophy

Atlas is designed around six core principles:

1. Customer Ownership
2. Security by Design
3. Operational Simplicity
4. Intelligent Automation
5. Scalability
6. Responsible Platform Integration

These principles guide product development, customer experience, platform security, and all integrations with third-party services, including Meta's platforms.

---

## Mission

Atlas exists to help organizations operate more efficiently through secure communications, intelligent workflows, and integrated business operations while respecting customer privacy, platform policies, and industry best practices.

Our goal is to build software that enables businesses to better serve their customers without compromising security, transparency, or ownership.
