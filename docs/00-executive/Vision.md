## Vision statement (draft)

Atlas AI will become the **trusted workflow and communication layer** for financial services agencies — connecting voluntary prospect engagement, compliant WhatsApp business messaging, and agent-led relationship management in one auditable platform.

### Pillars

1. **Human-first automation** — Technology assists licensed professionals; it does not replace fiduciary judgment.
2. **Opt-in communication** — Every conversation begins with user or business-initiated contact.
3. **Agency ownership** — Each customer owns their Meta assets, data, and client relationships; Atlas provides the engine.
4. **Compliance by design** — Business rules, audit logs, and policy-aware workflows are built in, not bolted on.
5. **Operational clarity** — Mission Control, Prospect Workspace, and executive dashboards give teams a single source of truth.

---

## What success looks like

- Agencies onboard their WhatsApp Business accounts in minutes via Embedded Signup.
- Prospects receive timely, relevant responses within Meta policy and business rules.
- Leadership sees pipeline health without spreadsheet drift.
- Meta and regulatory reviewers can trace messaging intent from public website to conversation log.
