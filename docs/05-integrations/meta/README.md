## Purpose

This folder contains the **complete Meta approval package** for Atlas AI — documentation prepared for:

- **Meta Business Verification**
- **WhatsApp Business Platform** review
- **Facebook Developer App Review**

The package explains what Atlas is, how Meta technologies are used, how user data is handled, and how the platform complies with Meta Business Messaging policies.

**Primary audience:** Meta reviewers who may not be familiar with Atlas AI or Team Vision Financial.
